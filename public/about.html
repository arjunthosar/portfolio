I am a photographer who focuses on capturing beautiful moments in nature and outdoors. I also enjoy capturing portraits of people and pictures of interesting architecture.

The Start

At 14 years old, I picked up my first camera (my smartphone) and got inspired by how astonishing the world looked within the frame of its lens. This led me to begin my journey in photography.

My Journey

Throughout the years, I have learned and self-taught many photography skills, and I have always strived to perfect my art of photography. Follow my journey on my Instagram.
